M1A2 Abrams, USA

Profile

Its firepower is slightly inferior to other tanks like the Leopard 2A7 due to its shorter 120 mm L44 smoothbore gun. Yet despite this, it is still a very dangerous war machine. Its multifuel turbine engine offers good performance but does require a lot of maintenance. The U.S. Army plans to keep the tank in service well into 2050.
