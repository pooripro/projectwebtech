T-14 Armata, Russia

Profile

The T-14 Armata is Russia's most advanced main battle tank. They plan to build around 130 of them with somewhere in the order of 20 having currently been delivered. This tank features many innovative features (most of which are classified), but its unmanned turret is leagues ahead of its rivals. The tank only requires three human crew members who are housed in an armored capsule in the front of the hull.
